Comprehensive Study Abroad Services

At Azigi Travel and Tour, we help students achieve their dreams of pursuing higher education abroad. From finding the right university to securing your student visa, we guide you through every step of the process to ensure your success.

Our Process

University Selection

We help you choose the best university and program that match your academic goals and preferences.

Document Preparation

We assist in preparing essential documents, including:

  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Valid passport
  • Proof of financial support
Application Submission

We guide you in completing and submitting university applications accurately and on time.

Visa Application Support

We assist with the student visa process, ensuring all requirements are met for a smooth approval.

Pre-departure Assistance

We provide guidance on accommodation, travel arrangements, and cultural adaptation for a stress-free start to your academic journey.
