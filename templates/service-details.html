Comprehensive Work Abroad Services

At Azigi Travel and Tour, we specialize in helping you achieve your dreams of working abroad by guiding you through every step of the process. From gathering the required documents to securing your work permit and visa, we ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Our Process

Document Preparation

We assist in gathering essential documents such as:

  • Scanned copy of your passport
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV) or resume
  • Proof of work experience or recommendations
  • Educational certificates and qualifications
  • Proof of funds (if applicable)
Job Matching

We connect you with reputable employers abroad, ensuring suitable placements tailored to your skills and experience.

Work Permit Application

We prepare and submit your work permit application, ensuring all requirements are met for approval.

Visa Processing

We handle the visa application process for a smooth transition to your destination country.

Travel Assistance

We provide travel arrangements and post-arrival support to ensure a stress-free journey.

Why Choose Us?

Our experienced team ensures all placements are legitimate, compliant with immigration laws, and tailored to your career aspirations. We work only with trusted employers to prioritize your safety and success.
